FISHeries Economics Explorer (FISHEyE)

Planned developments



FISHEyE is rather new and currently contains one application, the Net Revenue Explorer for catcher vessels. Our goal is to improve FISHEyE over time by enhancing and expanding the current application.

Please contact us at nwfsc.fisheye@noaa.gov if you experience any difficulty accessing or interacting with the site.

What's next on our list? We are working to add the catcher-processors, motherships, and first receivers and shorebased processors to the Net Revenue Explorer. We will also provide new tools for exploring a broad suite of economic performance metrics for the West Coast Groundfish Trawl Catch Share Program.
